How Virus Spread

How is the coronavirus contagious?

The coronavirus is contagious primarily through respiratory droplets when an infected person talks, coughs, or sneezes. It can also spread by touching a surface with the virus and then touching the face. Practicing good hand hygiene, wearing masks, maintaining physical distance, and getting vaccinated are key preventive measures.

  • Human Contact

    COVID-19 spreads through human contact via respiratory droplets. Practice social distancing and wear masks to mitigate the risk

  • Air Transmission

    COVID-19 can be transmitted through the air via respiratory droplets. Mitigate the risk by maintaining social distance and wearing masks.

  • Contaminated Objects

    COVID-19 can spread through contaminated objects. Regular handwashing and avoiding touching your face help minimize this risk.

Corona Virus Symptoms

  • Coughing And Sneezing

    Frequent coughing and sneezing may indicate potential COVID-19 symptoms. Stay vigilant and follow health guidelines if experienced.

  • Hot Fever

    Hot fever may be a symptom of COVID-19. Monitor your health and seek guidance if you experience elevated temperatures.

  • Strong Headache

    A strong headache can be a symptom of COVID-19. Pay attention to your health and seek advice if persistent.

  • Shortness Of Breath

    Shortness of breath may indicate a potential COVID-19 symptom. Seek medical advice if experiencing difficulty breathing.

  • Confusion

    Confusion can be a symptom of COVID-19. Monitor your health closely and consult with healthcare professionals if needed.

  • Sore Throat

    A sore throat is a common symptom of COVID-19. Stay vigilant and consider seeking medical advice if you experience this discomfort.

Prevention Coronavirus

Prevent the spread of coronavirus by practicing good hand hygiene, wearing masks, maintaining social distance, and getting vaccinated. Stay informed and follow public health guidelines for effective prevention.

  • Wash Your Hands For 20sec

    Combat the spread of germs: Wash your hands for 20 seconds with soap and water, a simple yet effective measure for personal hygiene.

  • Cover Nose and Mouth When Sneezing

    Help prevent the spread of germs: Cover your nose and mouth when sneezing to protect yourself and others from potential infection.

  • Wear A Mask If Available

    Prioritize safety: Wear a mask if available to protect yourself and others from the spread of COVID-19.
